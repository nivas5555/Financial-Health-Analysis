# Financial Health Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a **Financial Health Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze key financial metrics such as revenue, profitability, cash flow, budget variance, and receivables aging.

The dashboard is designed from a **business decision-making perspective**, helping stakeholders quickly understand financial performance across time, regions, and products/services.

---

## Business Objectives
- Track overall **revenue and profitability**
- Compare **actual revenue vs budget**
- Analyze **cash inflows, cash outflows, and net cash position**
- Monitor **receivables aging** to assess credit risk
- Evaluate **product/service performance**
- Compare **year-over-year revenue trends (2023 vs 2024)**

---

## Key Metrics & Visuals
- **Total Revenue**
- **Gross Margin %**
- **EBITDA %**
- **Net Cash Position**
- Revenue Trend by Month (2023 vs 2024)
- Monthly Revenue: Actual vs Budget
- Revenue by Product / Service
- Cash Flow Waterfall (Inflows → Outflows → Net Cash)
- Average Receivables Aging by Region
